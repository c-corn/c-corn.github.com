Fundamental Theorem of Algebra

Let n:nat and f be a complex polynomial of degree (S n).

Section FTA_reg'.

Variable f : cpoly_cring C.
Variable n : N.
Hypothesis f_degree : degree (S n) f.

Lemma FTA_reg' : {f1 : C[X] | degree 1 f1 | {f2 : C[X] | degree n f2 | f [=] f1[*]f2}}.








End FTA_reg'.
